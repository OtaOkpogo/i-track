i-track Services - Smart Vehicking Tracking Platform

I-track Services is a real-time GPS vehicle tracking platform designed for individuals, businesses, and fleet operators. The platform provides accurate, live monitoring of vehicles, performance insights, and powerful fleet management tools (yuntrack).

Key Features
  • Live GPS Tracking: Monitor vehicles in real time on an interactive map.
  • Movement History: View past trips and playback routes.
  • Smart Alerts: Overspeeding, geofence entry/exit, idling, SOS, and more.
  • Driver & Vehicle Management: Assign drivers, manage vehicle profiles, and track maintenance.
  • Analytics Dashboard: Mileage, fuel, performance reports, driver behavior, and fleet insights.
  • Geofencing: Create custom zones and get instant alerts.
  • Device Support: Works with major GPS tracker protocols (GT06, Concox, Teltonika, etc.).
Who Is It For?
  • Fleet companies
  • Logistics & delivery services
  • Transport operators
  • Car rental companies
  • Personal vehicle owners
